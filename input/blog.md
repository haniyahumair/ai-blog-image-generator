# 5 Signs You Need Addiction Treatment

Addiction affects millions of people worldwide. Studies show that 1 in 7 Americans 
will face a substance use disorder in their lifetime. Recovery is possible with the 
right treatment and support system.

Key signs include:
- Inability to stop using despite wanting to
- Neglecting responsibilities at work or home
- Withdrawal symptoms when not using
- Using more than intended
- Continuing use despite negative consequences

Treatment options include therapy, medication-assisted treatment, and support groups.
Mental health plays a crucial role in recovery success.
